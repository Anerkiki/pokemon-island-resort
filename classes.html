Flying Skills

with Trainer Kiawe & Charizard
or Trainer Mallow & Flygon

Join Trainer Kiawe and Charizard or Trainer Mallow and Flygon for an exhilarating flying experience. This class focuses on mastering aerial maneuvers, improving coordination, and building trust between you and your Pokémon.

You'll learn essential flying techniques, including takeoff, mid-air control, and safe landings. You'll also gain insights into advanced flying techniques, such as navigating through challenging weather conditions and performing aerial tricks to impress your audience.

Whether you're a beginner or looking to enhance your flying skills, this class offers a perfect blend of adventure and learning. Soar through the skies and create unforgettable memories with your Pokémon!

Ocean Riding & Water Skills

with Trainer Lana & Lapras

Join Trainer Lana and Lapras for an unforgettable ocean adventure. This class focuses on developing your Pokémon's water skills while enjoying the serene beauty of the ocean.

You'll learn essential techniques for riding on water, including balance, coordination, and communication with your Pokémon. Trainer Lana will guide you through exercises designed to build confidence and trust between you and your aquatic companion.

Whether you're a beginner or looking to enhance your water-riding abilities, this class offers a perfect blend of fun and skill-building. Don't miss the chance to create unforgettable memories while exploring the wonders of the ocean!

Beach & Dune Riding

with Trainer Kiawe or
Trainer Mallow & Tauros

Join Trainer Kiawe or Trainer Mallow for an exciting adventure in beach and dune riding. This class focuses on mastering the art of riding across sandy terrains with the help of Tauros, a Pokémon known for its strength and stability on uneven surfaces.

In addition to exploring the dunes, you'll also have the opportunity to ride through shallow ocean waters, experiencing the thrill of splashing waves while honing your riding skills.

Whether you're looking to improve your riding skills or simply enjoy a thrilling experience, this class offers a perfect blend of fun and learning. Get ready to explore the scenic beaches, dunes, and ocean while building a stronger bond with your Pokémon!

Fire Breathing & Fire Skills

with Trainer Kiawe & Charizard

Join Trainer Kiawe and his fiery companion Charizard for an exhilarating experience in mastering fire skills. This class is designed to teach you and your Pokémon the art of fire breathing, flame control, and advanced fire techniques in a safe and controlled environment.

You'll also learn how to use fire effectively in various scenarios, from creating dazzling displays to practical survival skills. Trainer Kiawe will guide you through step-by-step exercises, ensuring you and your Pokémon build confidence and control over their fiery abilities.

Whether you're a beginner looking to ignite your first flame or an experienced trainer aiming to refine your fire-based abilities, this class offers a unique opportunity to learn from the best. Unleash your inner fire and take your skills to the next level!

Snorkeling & Deep Sea Diving

with Trainer Lana & Primarina

Dive into the crystal-clear waters with Trainer Lana and Primarina. This class offers an unforgettable underwater adventure where you'll master snorkeling techniques and deep-sea dive to explore vibrant coral reefs teeming with marine life and Pokémon. You'll even have the chance to swim alongside majestic whale Pokémon, making for a truly magical experience. Perfect for beginners or experienced divers, this class ensures a fascinating experience!
