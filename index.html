Our Island Activities

Beach Entertainment

Immerse yourself in the vibrant energy of our beach entertainment, where the fun never ends. Join us for lively beach parties featuring music, dancing, and unforgettable moments.

At night, witness the mesmerizing art of fire dancing performed by skilled trainers and their Pokémon, creating a spectacle of light and movement.

Take a refreshing dip in the crystal-clear waters or indulge in luxurious pamper sessions at our beachfront spa, designed to rejuvenate both trainers and Pokémon.

Book one of our Luxury Sea View Penthouses to enjoy private access to the beach and a balcony with breathtaking views, making your stay truly magical.

Hot Springs and Fishing Pools

Immerse yourself in the soothing embrace of our natural hot springs and tranquil fishing pools, surrounded by breathtaking views of the island's lush landscapes and serene atmosphere.

Our hot springs are infused with minerals that promote relaxation and rejuvenation, offering the perfect retreat for both trainers and their Pokémon after a day of adventure. The fishing pools provide a peaceful spot to unwind and try your hand at catching a variety of island Pokémon or simply enjoy the calming waters.

Book one of our Mountain View Apartments to enjoy easy access to these relaxing amenities, where you can unwind in tranquility and connect with nature.

Berry Picking, Foraging & Cooking

Immerse yourself in the joy of berry picking within our expansive greenery and vibrant flora. Our trainers will guide you in harvesting a variety of rare and exotic berries, fresh mushrooms and vegetables from the island's lush gardens. Each ingredient has unique properties to enhance Pokémon training, and well-being.

You and your Pokémon can prepare delicious meals on the beach using your freshly picked berries, mushrooms, and vegetables — making every meal a fun and memorable experience.

Book one of our Garden View Chalets with access to your own private garden where you can pick berries and vegetanbles at your leisure, relax in the tranquil beauty of nature, and interact with our friendly grass Pokémon.

Arena Battles

Experience the thrill of Pokémon battles in our state-of-the-art arena, where trainers and their Pokémon can showcase their skills in friendly competitions.

Our arena features dynamic environments that simulate various terrains, providing an exciting and immersive experience for both participants and spectators.

Join us for daily shows, where our expert trainers demonstrate advanced battle techniques and strategies, or sign up for our interactive workshops to learn how to enhance your own Pokémon's abilities.

Book one of our Arena View Apartments to enjoy a front-row seat to all the action from the comfort of your private balcony.
