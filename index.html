Berry Picking

Immerse yourself in the joy of berry picking within our expansive greenery and vibrant flora, creating a serene and picturesque environment.

Our trainers will guide you in discovering a variety of rare and exotic berries, each with unique properties to enhance Pokémon training and care.

For an exclusive experience, book our Garden View Chalet with access to your own private garden where you can pick berries at your leisure, relax in the tranquil beauty of nature, and interact with our friendly grass Pokémon.

Pokémon Arena Battles

Experience the thrill of Pokémon battles in our state-of-the-art arena, where trainers and their Pokémon can showcase their skills in friendly competitions.

Our arena features dynamic environments that simulate various terrains, providing an exciting and immersive experience for both participants and spectators.

Join us for daily shows, where our expert trainers demonstrate advanced battle techniques and strategies, or sign up for our interactive workshops to learn how to enhance your own Pokémon's abilities.

For an exclusive experience, book our Arena View Apartment to enjoy a front-row seat to all the action from the comfort of your private balcony.
